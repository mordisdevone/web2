Renewable Energy

Agriculture

The long awaited Next generation of WSV3 nearing launch day

WSV3 founder Paul Maravelias narrates visual update on March 2023 V6.0 development progress - 30th, Mar 2023

WSV3 Version 6, under development since October 2022, is set to revolutionize weather graphics software. It aims to strike a balance between practicality and aesthetics in weather data visualization, avoiding both the extremes of flashy style and outdated, flat digital maps. The goal is to ensure that the visual representations are scientifically robust, yet also promote intuitive clarity for the user. To do this, WSV3 Version 6 is being re-engineered with a focus on high-performance, real-time rendering techniques similar to those used in blockbuster video games. This approach ensures a smooth 60 FPS framerate on standard hardware. The software is designed to be highly efficient and built with native software design principles. This is a deliberate move away from the prevalent trend of heavy, inefficient, non-native web browser applications. - Paul Maravelias, WSV3 Founder and Lifetime Developer.

Comprehensive Satellite Imagery

WSV3 provides fast-downloading, animated GOES Satellite imagery with a choice of 8 different products, linked to a unified animated timeline for comprehensive weather insights.

Advanced Radar Data Viewer

Outperforming competitors, WSV3 includes a world-class NEXRAD Level 2/3 radar data viewer, featuring real-time, LiveScan™ and LiveComposite™ technologies.

METAR Display Engine

WSV3 showcases nine parameters from over 8,000+ global stations, fully animated on the main timeline with an unprecedented temporal interpolation feature.

Tropical Tracking Products

With a full suite of tropical tracking products, WSV3 displays a wealth of system tracking information in a visually appealing and efficient display.

National Radar Composite

WSV3's new raster data system enables quick loading of quality-controlled, precip-typed radar composites from our cloud-backed servers.

Customizable Model Data

Inspect every GRIB2 parameter of any standard NOAA NCEP operational weather forecast model. WSV3's model engine is fully customizable and extensible, offering rapid-loading inspection of static or animated forecast model data parameters.
